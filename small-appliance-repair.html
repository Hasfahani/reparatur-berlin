    FAQ

    Questions about small-appliance repair in Berlin.

    Is it worth repairing a small appliance?

    That depends on the appliance type, age and fault. High-quality bean-to-cup coffee machines, good kitchen machines and solid vacuum cleaners are often worth repairing. With very cheap appliances, replacement may make more sense. We will tell you honestly after inspection.

    My microwave no longer heats. Is it repairable?

    Yes, many microwave faults are repairable, including failed magnetrons, blown fuses or faulty door interlocks. Repairability and effort become clear after diagnosis.

    Do I need to send the appliance in or do you come to me?

    Some small appliances can be brought in directly. For larger or heavier units, we discuss the best option after the first enquiry. On-site appointments in Berlin are also possible where appropriate.

    Which brands do you repair for small appliances?

    We work with all common brands including Bosch, Siemens, Braun, Philips, DeLonghi, Miele, AEG, Krups, WMF and more.

    What should I include in my enquiry?

    Helpful details are the appliance type, brand and model, a short fault description, your Berlin district and the best time for a callback.
