Why repair?

Repair is often worth it, especially with cooling appliances.

Cooling appliances last a long time

Fridges and freezers are durable and often last 15 to 20 years. A repair can extend that lifespan for a fraction of replacement cost.

Many faults are well understood

Problems such as thermostats, door seals and defrost components are often straightforward to diagnose and can be cost-effective to repair.

Avoid replacement hassle

Buying new often means delivery delays, installation and disposal of the old appliance. A repaired fridge can go straight back into daily use.

Honest recommendation

If the repair is not worth it, we say so directly. No pressure, no hidden diagnostic fees, just a clear recommendation you can trust.

    FAQ

    Questions about fridge repair in Berlin.

    My fridge is not cooling anymore. Repair or replace?

    That depends on the exact fault and the age of the appliance. Common causes such as a faulty thermostat or blocked condenser are often quite repairable and affordable. We will assess the issue and give you an honest answer.

    Why is so much ice building up inside my fridge?

    Heavy ice build-up often points to a damaged door seal, a defrost issue or a problem in the cooling system. These are usually diagnosable and often fixable without replacing the appliance.

    Do you also repair freezers and fridge-freezer combinations?

    Yes. We work with freestanding fridges, freezers, fridge-freezer combinations and built-in cooling appliances.

    Which brands do you repair?

    All common brands including Liebherr, Bosch, Siemens, Miele, AEG, Samsung, LG, Bauknecht, Gorenje, Beko, Haier and more.

    What should I include in my enquiry?

    Helpful details are the brand and model, year if known, a short fault description, your Berlin district and the best time for a callback.
