Why repair?

Repair instead of replacement makes particular sense for built-in appliances.

Built-in replacements are expensive

Replacing a built-in oven or hob often means extra kitchen work as well. Repair keeps the existing appliance in place without unnecessary rebuilds.

Many faults can be repaired directly

Heating elements, thermostats and control units can often be replaced precisely without changing the entire appliance.

Honest assessment first

Every recommendation begins with a clear diagnosis. If the repair is not worth it, we tell you openly.

Back in use faster

Delivery times for new built-in appliances can take weeks. A repair often gets your oven working again much sooner.

    FAQ

    Questions about oven repair in Berlin.

    My oven no longer heats up. What could be wrong?

    Common causes include a failed heating element, faulty thermostat or broken control unit. All three are potentially repairable. The diagnosis shows exactly what has failed.

    Do you also repair induction hobs?

    Yes. We inspect induction, ceramic and classic electric hobs. With induction, repairability depends strongly on the exact fault and we clarify that after diagnosis.

    Which brands do you repair?

    All common brands including Bosch, Siemens, Neff, Miele, AEG, Gaggenau, Samsung, Gorenje, Bauknecht and more.

    Do you also work on built-in appliances?

    Yes. Built-in ovens and hobs are inspected on site. In most cases, full removal is not necessary for the diagnosis.

    What should I include in my enquiry?

    Helpful details are the brand and model, whether the appliance is built-in or freestanding, a short fault description or error code, your Berlin district and the best time for a callback.
