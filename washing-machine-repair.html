Why repair?

Repair instead of replacement is often the better choice.

Save money

In many cases a repair costs far less than buying a new appliance, especially if the machine is under 10 years old and spare parts are still available.

Save resources

Repairing an appliance avoids the manufacturing, transport and disposal footprint of a replacement. It is easier on your budget and on the environment.

Honest diagnosis first

Every recommendation starts with a clear assessment. If the repair is not worth it, we say so directly, with no hidden diagnostic surprises.

Keep the appliance you know

Berlin households value reliability. If your machine has served you well, repairing it is often better than taking the risk of replacing it too soon.

    FAQ

    Questions about washing machine repair in Berlin.

    Is it worth repairing an older washing machine?

    That depends on the age, model and type of fault. As a rule of thumb, if repair costs stay below about 50 to 60 percent of the price of a new machine and the appliance is under 10 years old, repair is usually worth it. We will give you a clear recommendation after diagnosis.

    Do you come directly to my home?

    Yes. For most washing machine faults, an on-site appointment in Berlin makes sense because the appliance is not easy to transport. The exact next step is agreed after your first enquiry.

    Which brands do you repair?

    We work with all common brands including Bosch, Siemens, Miele, AEG, Samsung, LG, Bauknecht, Whirlpool, Gorenje, Indesit, Beko and more. If you are unsure, just ask.

    What does a diagnosis cost?

    Diagnosis and repair costs are discussed after the initial assessment. There are no hidden fees. You will know what to expect before anything moves forward.

    What should I include in my enquiry?

    Helpful details are the brand and model, year if known, a short fault description or error code, your Berlin district and the best time for a callback.
