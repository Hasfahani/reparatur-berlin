    FAQ

    Questions about dishwasher repair in Berlin.

    Is it worth repairing my dishwasher?

    That depends on the age, model and type of fault. Many common issues such as pumps, heating elements and door seals are quite repairable and often cost-effective. We will give you a clear recommendation after diagnosis.

    Can you repair built-in dishwashers too?

    Yes. We work on both freestanding and integrated appliances. Access to the unit is often important for diagnosis, so we coordinate that with you in advance.

    Which brands do you repair?

    All common brands including Bosch, Siemens, Miele, AEG, Bauknecht, Whirlpool, Samsung, LG, Gorenje, Neff and more.

    What does the error code on my display mean?

    Error codes such as E4, E15 or F11 usually point toward specific components such as heating, the pump or water intake. If you include the code in your enquiry, we can speed up the assessment.

    What should I include in my enquiry?

    Helpful details are the brand and model, the displayed error code if there is one, a short fault description, your Berlin district and the best time for a callback.
